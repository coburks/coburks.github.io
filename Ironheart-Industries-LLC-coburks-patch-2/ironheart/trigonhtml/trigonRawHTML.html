About Us

Trigon United was founded in 2006 and offers responsive, customer focused service that’s second to none. Our team of experienced professionals are specialists in exterior cleaning, pressure washing, concrete cleaning, concrete repair, asphalt repair, surface repair, surface removal and a wide array of traffic markings.

 

In 2018 Trigon United acquired Renue Systems of SE Texas. This acquisition has allowed us to greatly expand upon our suite of offerings – as we can now provide many services inside of properties – including stonecare, bio remediation, janitorial services, flood damage, PTAC Units, Odor Abatement, and much more. However of particular note, we also offer COVID-19 disinfection via electrostatic spray.

 

For COVID-19 – we use state of the art technology to disperse CDC approved disinfectants to keep you and yours safe. (It will also kill a wide variety of other bacteria and viruses!) It works by breaking down regular droplets into much smaller micron sized droplets. Additionally, it will electrostatically charge them, which will cause the droplets to seek objects and surfaces more readily while being evenly dispersed. All while being perfectly safe for humans, pets, and electronics. The mist will get inside hard to reach areas you might not have even thought about.

 

What’s more, we’re able to schedule service 24 hours a day, seven days a week to accommodate your needs. So when you want it done right, to code, on time and on budget, turn to Trigon United. We guarantee your satisfaction or we’ll make it right.
